About

This Reinforced Concrete Design (RCD) calculator performs analysis and design of rectangular beam sections per NSCP 2015, using Working Stress Design (WSD) and Ultimate Strength Design (USD). Use it for coursework or preliminary design. Always verify critical results with licensed engineers and official codes.
